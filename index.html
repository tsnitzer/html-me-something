Hoya carnosa 'Krimson Queen'

Lil' cutie with pink or white variegation on the outer leaf margins. Has been super slow growing.

Hoya lanceolata ssp. bella

Bought as a tiny cutting. Currently rooting in leca. Looks happy enough. Has one new baby leaf slowly unfurling. Has been munched by the cat.

Monstera adansonii

Have at least four of these around. Two are rooting - one in water, and one in leca. V easy v cute.

Monstera deliciosa var. borsigiana albo-variegata

Cash cow. Main plant from a few years ago is growing so wonky but is happy. Attempting air layering with a second cutting to sell.

Monstera siltepecana

First plant ordered online! It's super pretty. Kind of sparkly, silvery leaves. Big fan. Potted up recently in November. 10/10.

Philodendron cordatum

Got her over the summer and she has been growing in a vase of water since then. Easy and cool.

Philodendron hederaceum 'Micans'

Have several cuttings from work + friends. They're all rooting well in water. They are gorg and I want them everywhere.

Rhaphidophora tetrasperma

Bought from pal Steph. Really determined to make this huge. Seems okay, has put out a few new leaves.

Rhipsalis baccifera

Cute! Easy! Trailing epiphytic cactus.

Scindapsus pictus 'Argyraeus'

These are literally all over my apartment and rooting in various cups of water. New leaves are so pretty. As a whole, kind of underwhelming, but is a really pretty filler plant.
